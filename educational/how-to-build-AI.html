How to build AI for dummies

Introduction

Artificial Intelligence is the most booming field and your idea is going to take the market by storm. Now you are talking to an AI engineer to make your dream a reality and you have no idea what they are talking about. Let me briefly introduce to you some of the steps that are required to build the best AI possible.

Step 1: Determining an objective

The first step of any project is to determine the goal of the Artificial Intelligence. Intelligence doesn't magically form out of thin air, as the hype might make it seem. Rather, it is deliberately designed to solve a specific objective, such as the recognition of faces, forecasting of weather, or a specific use of GPT. Without a clear definition of what the AI needs to be able to do, it is not possible to design nor train one. For this reason, an AI engineer will typically first ask questions about the nature of the problem in order to conceive a strategy to solve it.
